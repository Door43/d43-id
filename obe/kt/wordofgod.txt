====== firman Allah, firman Yahweh, firman Tuhan, kitab suci ======

===== Definisi: =====

Istilah "firman Allah" mengacu pada apapun yang telah Allah komunikasikan pada manusia. Termasuk pesan lisan maupun tulisan.

  * Bertahun-tahun setelah firman Allah dituliskan, orang masih bisa membaca kitab suci ini dan mengenal ajaran Allah.
  * Istilah "kitab suci" merujuk secara khusus kepada pesan tertulis yang Allah suruh orang-orang tuliskan.
  * Istilah terkait "firman Yahweh" dan "firman Tuhan" seringkali merujuk pada pesan tertentu dari Allah yang diberikan kepada seorang nabi atau orang lain dalam Alkitab.
  * Kadang-kadang muncul hanya sebagai "firman" atau "firmanku" atau "firmanmu" (ketika berbicara tentang firman Allah).
  * Dalam Perjanjian Baru, Yesus disebut "Firman" dan "Firman Allah." Gelar-gelar ini berarti bahwa Yesus sepenuhnya mengungkapkan siapa Allah, karena ia sendiri adalah Allah.
===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * Depending on the context, other ways to translate this term could include, "the message of Yahweh" or "God's message" or "the teachings from God."
  * It may be more natural in some languages to make this term plural and say "God's words" or "the words of Yahweh."
  * The expression "the word of God (or Yahweh) came" is often used to introduce something that God told his prophet or his people. This could be translated as, "Yahweh spoke these words" or "God spoke this message."
  * The term "scripture" or "scriptures" could be translated as "the writings" or "the written message from God." This term should be translated differently from the translation of the term "word."
  * When "word" occurs alone, it could be translated as "message" or "God's word" or "teachings," depending on the context. Also consider the alternate translations suggested above.
